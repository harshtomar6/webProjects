Steve Jobs

The Greatest Innovator and Visionary



Here's a Timeline of Steve Jobs Life:


  • 1955- Born on February 25, 1955.
  • 1961- Moved to Mountain View, California.
  • 1968- Went to attend Homestead High School.
  • 1971- Went to University of California, Berkley.
  • 1974- Travels to India for spiritual Enlightment.
  • 1976- Invents the Historic Apple 1 Computer with his childhood friend Steve Wozniak.
  • 1977- Intoduces world the Apple 2 Computer at the West Coast Computer Fair
  • 1980- Worth estimates more than 100 million Dollars.
  • 1983- Apple Computers introduces the Macintosh.
  • 1985- Fired from his own Company.
  • 1986- Founded NeXt Computers and funded Disney.
  • 1997- Brought back to the Apple Computers.
  • 1998- Introduced the First iMac Computer.
  • 2001- First generation of iPod was released making a revolution in Music Industry.
  • 2003- Diagnosed with cancerous tumor in his pancreas.
  • 2007- First iPhone was released.
  • 2010- First iPad was released.
  • 2011- On August 2011, Jobs announced his Resignation as Apple's CEO.
  • 2011- On October 2011, Jobs died at his Palo Alto, California home
