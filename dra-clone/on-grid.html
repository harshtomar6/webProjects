ON-Grid solar power plant

The grid connect inverter converts the DC electricity produced by the solar panels into AC electricity, which can be used by the home appliances.

If a grid connect system is producing more power than is being consumed, the surplus is fed into the mains power grid to Govt via net-meter. Govt will adjust the same in your next electricity bill.
